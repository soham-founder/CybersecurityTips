Welcome to Cybersecurity Tips

In today's digital world, staying safe online is more important than ever. This page provides simple tips to help you protect yourself from common cyber threats.

Passwords

🔒

Use Strong Passwords

Create passwords that are long, unique, and include a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.

🔑

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security by enabling 2FA on your accounts whenever possible.

🗝️

Use a Password Manager

Store and manage your passwords securely with a trusted password manager.

Emails & Phishing

📧

Verify Senders

Always check the email address of the sender before clicking links or opening attachments.

🚫

Avoid Suspicious Links

Do not click on links from unknown sources. Hover over them to check the URL.

🛡️

Report Phishing

If you receive a phishing email, report it to your email provider and delete it.

Social Media Safety

👥

Adjust Privacy Settings

Review and set your social media profiles to private to control who sees your posts.

🔒

Be Careful with Sharing

Think before sharing personal information online, as it can be used against you.

🚷

Accept Friend Requests Wisely

Only accept friend requests from people you know in real life.

Device Security

🔄

Keep Software Updated

Regularly update your operating system and apps to fix security vulnerabilities.

🛡️

Use Antivirus Software

Install and run antivirus software to protect against malware and viruses.

🔐

Lock Your Devices

Use passwords or biometrics to lock your devices when not in use.
